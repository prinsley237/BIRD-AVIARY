How to acquire Parrots?

Thinking of bringing a parrot into your home? Always choose ethical and legal sources. Experts recommend adopting from licensed breeders, reputable pet stores, or bird rescue centers. Avoid illegal trade — not only is it harmful to bird populations, it’s also punishable by law..

Cost of Parrots and others

Looking to buy a parrot? Prices vary widely based on species, age, and origin. Common species like Budgies start at around $25–$50, while African Greys and Macaws can range from $1,000 to over $3,000. Don't forget to budget for cages, toys, food, and vet care — owning a parrot is a long-term commitment!
